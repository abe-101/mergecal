Why MergeCal?

MergeCal expertly consolidates sports, corporate, and rental event schedules into a single streamlined calendar, enhancing accessibility and coordination. This integration allows teams, businesses, and property managers to operate more efficiently, reducing conflicts and fostering transparency.

Meet the Creator

Abe Hanoka, the creator of MergeCal.org and MyHebrewDates.com, is a seasoned full-stack developer renowned for his ability to simplify complex processes through innovative software solutions. A self-taught and self-driven problem solver, Abe has effectively used his extensive expertise in Python, Django, and open-source software to develop tools that enhance productivity and process efficiency. His notable contributions to community projects and his role as maintainer of dj-stripe highlight his commitment to improving software usability and accessibility. Abe's work, particularly in creating solutions like MergeCal, supports diverse user groups ranging from sports teams and educational administrators to freelancers and rental coordinators, ensuring they manage their schedules with ease and efficiency.
